Common questions

Why is this cheaper than hiring in-house?

Each tier costs roughly one-third of the salary bill of the equivalent team — because our pods are AI-leveraged. You also skip hiring risk, management overhead, and tooling costs.

What's the 90-day promise?

Measurable movement on agreed leading indicators by day 90, or we work free until there is. Conditions in the MSA — mainly that agreed client dependencies (approvals, access) are met.

Payment terms?

Retainers advance-billed on the 1st. Projects 50/30/20. UPI, NEFT, cards via Razorpay. GST invoices, e-invoicing supported.

Why don't you publish fixed prices?

Every engagement is scoped to your business after a short free audit, then quoted in writing before anything starts — so you pay for the system you actually need, not a rate-card bundle.
