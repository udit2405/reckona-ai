About Reckona AI

The firm we
wished existed.

India's mid-market is caught between two bad options. Big-4 digital arms charge ₹2 crore to produce a roadmap. Freelancers charge ₹2 lakh to produce a website. Nobody sells what companies actually need: working AI systems plus the growth engine to feed them — at mid-market prices, with enterprise discipline.

So we built it. Senior strategists who write their own automations. Designers who understand conversion. Engineers who read P&Ls. Small pods, AI-leveraged, shipping every week. We run our own company on the exact systems we sell — our funnel, our reporting, our operations. This website, its lead capture, and the dashboards behind it are the live demo.
